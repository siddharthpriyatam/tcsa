Got Questions?
CLUCKING
FAQS.

We get a lot of questions. Most of them are about chicken. Here are the ones about the job.

Do I really need an engineering degree for Brand Manager? +
Yes. We know it sounds unusual for a brand role, but TCSA operates at the intersection of commerce, logistics, and digital infrastructure. We've found that engineers approach brand problems differently — with systems thinking and rigour. It works for us.
Why do you prefer unemployed candidates? +
We're building something important and we need people who can give it their full attention. Candidates juggling notice periods and two jobs don't start well. We'd rather wait for someone fully ready than rush someone who's half-committed.
Are you serious about the Carnatic music thing? +
Completely serious. Carnatic music teaches structured creativity, complex pattern memory, and deep focus under pressure. These are exactly the skills we need. Our co-founder is a grade 8 Veena player. The correlation between musical discipline and excellent software architecture is not a coincidence.
Is this a real organisation? Do you actually sell chicken? +
The Telangana Chicken Shop Association represents thousands of independent poultry retailers and wet market vendors across all 33 districts of Telangana. We don't sell chicken ourselves — we represent and support the people who do. Think of us as a chamber of commerce, but for poultry and with better branding.
What does a typical day look like as an intern? +
Real work from day one. No coffee runs. No sorting files. You'll be embedded in an actual project with deadlines, stakeholder meetings, and deliverables that ship to real users. We treat interns as junior full-timers — with support, but also responsibility.
Do I need to eat chicken to work here? +
No. We have vegetarian team members who are excellent at their jobs and deeply committed to the mission. You do need to respect the people who depend on this industry for their livelihoods. That's a non-negotiable.
